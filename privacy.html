Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

At Escape Road, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you play our game on this website.

1. Information We Collect

While using Escape Road, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Non-personal data such as browser type, device information, and game statistics
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve gameplay experience
  • Personal information (such as email) only if you voluntarily contact us

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for purposes such as:

  • Improving game performance and features
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Analyzing site traffic and gameplay trends
  • Ensuring compliance with legal requirements

3. Cookies

Escape Road uses cookies to enhance your browsing and gaming experience. Cookies help us remember preferences and measure performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (such as analytics or ad networks) to improve the service. These third parties may collect anonymous information through cookies or similar technologies.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Ask for corrections or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of cookies and data tracking

6. Children’s Privacy

Escape Road is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover that we have accidentally collected such data, it will be removed immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date at the top.
