Cars in Escape Road – Unlock, Upgrade, and Choose Your Perfect Ride

In Escape Road, your vehicle choice dramatically influences your performance. Each car has unique characteristics: speed, handling, and durability. Escape Road classifies vehicles into four distinct tiers: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. This guide will help you understand each type, how to unlock them, and choose the best ride for your playstyle.

Types of Cars

Here’s a breakdown of each vehicle tier:

  • Common: Basic cars that are easy to handle and great for beginners. They have moderate speed and durability, making them perfect for learning traffic patterns and controls.
  • Rare: Faster cars with slightly better handling and performance. Ideal for players who are comfortable with basic mechanics and want to improve their scores.
  • Epic: High-performance vehicles with superior speed, handling, and durability. Epic cars are suited for advanced players aiming to maximize survival time and points.
  • Legendary: The most powerful and rare vehicles in Escape Road. Legendary cars combine top speed, exceptional control, and durability, offering the ultimate advantage in high-risk chases.

How to Unlock New Cars

Unlocking cars depends on tier and achievement:

  • Common: Available from the start or unlocked with minimal points.
  • Rare: Achieved by reaching mid-level high scores or completing simple challenges.
  • Epic: Requires significant in-game achievements or coin collection.
  • Legendary: Reserved for top players who reach elite milestones or complete advanced challenges.

Vehicle Handling Tips

Each tier requires different driving strategies:

  • Common cars: Stable, forgiving—good for practicing maneuvers.
  • Rare cars: Slightly faster—practice smooth drifting and precise timing.
  • Epic cars: High-speed control is essential; mistakes are costly.
  • Legendary cars: Maximize their speed while avoiding obstacles; aggressive play yields rewards.

Choosing the Right Car for You

Consider these factors when selecting a vehicle:

  • Skill level: Beginners may prefer Common or Rare cars.
  • Playstyle: Aggressive players may benefit from Epic or Legendary vehicles.
  • Challenge type: Certain maps or missions may favor a particular tier.

Upgrades and Enhancements

Some cars, especially Rare, Epic, and Legendary, can be upgraded to enhance speed, handling, or durability. Focus upgrades on vehicles you use frequently to maximize performance. Combine this with strategies from our How to Play Escape Road guide for best results.
